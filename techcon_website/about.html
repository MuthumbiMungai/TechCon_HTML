Our History

TechCon was founded in 2010 as a grassroots meetup for local tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into an international event bringing together top talent, companies, and visionaries shaping the future of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to empower developers, creators, and innovators by providing a platform for learning, networking, and collaboration. We believe in technology as a tool for social good and global impact.

Past Speakers

Each year, we welcome thought leaders from across the globe to share their expertise and vision.
